How to Boil Rice

  1. Wash the desired amount of rice until the water going through is clear
  2. Place the washed rice in a pot and add cups of water proportionate to the rice
  3. Place the pot over the stove with the stove being lit
  4. Reduce the heat to medium and let it simmer, cover the pot and wait for about 15 minutes
  5. Turn the heat off and serve however way when cooled
